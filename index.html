Starbucks Pumpkin Spice Latte

Pumpkin Spice Latte Recipe

Ingredients: 2 cups milk, 2 tbsp pumpkin puree, 1-2 tbsp sugar, 1 tbsp vanilla extract, 1/2 tsp pumpkin pie spice, 1-2 shots of espresso, whipped cream (optional)

Instructions: Heat milk, pumpkin puree, and sugar in a saucepan until hot. Stir in vanilla and pumpkin pie spice. Pour mixture into a mug over espresso. Top with whipped cream and a sprinkle of pumpkin pie spice.

Starbucks Mocha Frappuccino

Mocha Frappuccino Recipe

Ingredients: 1 cup strong brewed coffee, 1 cup milk, 2 tbsp sugar, 2 tbsp chocolate syrup, 1 cup ice, whipped cream (optional)

Instructions: Blend coffee, milk, sugar, chocolate syrup, and ice until smooth. Pour into a glass and top with whipped cream if desired.
